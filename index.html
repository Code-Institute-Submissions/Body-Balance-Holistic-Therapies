Therapies

At Body Balance Holistic Therapies, I provide individually tailored massage treatments as per the client’s issues and needs so to improve general health and wellbeing. Before each treatment, I go through the consultation form with the client focusing on the health history and current symptoms and issues.

Holistic therapies can provide immediate relief for muscle pain, improve sleep quality, blood and lymph circulation, muscle tension, digestion, etc. Regular massage is important for general wellbeing and prevention of possible muscular and other issues.

Holistic Massage

The term "holistic" derives from the ancient Greek word "holos," which means "whole." By definition, holistic massage treats the whole of the body as a single unity. This form of therapy helps by relaxing the muscles, calming the mind, and rejuvenating the spirit. It is one of the most effective ways to allow the body’s natural healing abilities to come forth and balance the entire body as a whole. This therapeutic treatment can be adapted to each client’s unique needs, physical characteristics, and personality.

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is a full body massage therapy which works on the soft tissues and the muscles to restore health by promoting calming and balancing effect on the nervous system. Swedish massage is one of the most commonly offered massage techniques which aims to promote relaxation by releasing muscle tension. Swedish massage can be gentler than deep tissue massage which can be preferred by people interested in relaxation and tension relief.

Sports Massage

Sports massage is the use of massage for the treatment and prevention of sports massage injuries. It can be used as part of a training program to help prevent injury, as part of rehabilitation program to treat injury, as part of warm-up for an event and as part of the wind-down after the event. Sports massage helps improve the suppleness and flexibility of muscles and joints. It improves lymph and blood circulation and assist body system in functioning at their optimum level.

Reflexology

Reflexology is a therapy that treats the feet in order to balance the whole body. Its main principle is that by massaging and applying pressure to the reflex areas, other corresponding and connected areas of the body will feel the benefit. The aim is to stimulate the body’s own healing processes in order to keep it in homeostasis. Reflexology encourages both relaxation and stimulation: the first helps the body rebuild its energy resources and recover from illness and diseases whereas the second helps activate the circulation and the nervous system, making both more efficient and thus benefiting the body as a whole.

Reiki

Reiki means universal life force energy in Japanese and Reiki healers act as channels for this universal energy to pass into the client. By using hands in certain positions on different parts of the body, the healer is said to draw energy to the body promoting healing, balance, and relaxation. It helps in restoring balance and harmony to the mind, body, and spirit.

Indian Head Massage

Indian Head Massage is based on the ancient Indian healing system called Ayurveda – the science of life. It is known to be the earliest recorded form of medical treatment dating back for thousands of years. It has played a very important role in Indian life as an important way of relieving pain and of promoting healing. A traditional form of head massage using specific techniques covering only the head, neck, and upper back. This treatment is very effective in treating stress, headaches, and eye strains. The treatment is performed by having the client seated in an upright position with the feet firmly on the ground.

Hot and Cold Stone Massage

The use of heated and cooled stones applied through massage or indirect application to the body to relax and rejuvenate muscles and circulation. The different temperatures stimulate the body’s natural healing responses to balance mind, body, and spirit. Before the treatment, stones will be sanitized and heated up to in a bath of 45 to 50 degree Celsius water. The stones are made of basalt, a black volcanic rock that is well known for its ability to absorb and retains heat.

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage is a type of massage therapy specifically tailored to the needs of a pregnant women. The therapy helps to reduce stress hormones, soothe nervous system, and loosen muscles. It can also increase blood flow, which is so important when you're pregnant, and keep your lymphatic system working at peak efficiency, flushing out toxins from your body. Prenatal massages are adapted for the anatomical changes you go through during pregnancy. Prenatal massages are generally considered safe after the first trimester, as long as you get the permission from your general practitioner and you disclose it during the consultation that you’re pregnant.

Lymphatic Drain Massage

Lymphatic drainage massage is a system of massage techniques that help the lymphatic system to function effectively. It is a gentle form of massage that uses superficial, pumping movements on the skin in the direction of the lymph nodes, the heart and ultimately into the systemic circulation. The lymphatic system plays a vital role in the body by regulating the immune system. Stress and deficient immune systems can make people susceptible to disease and infections. The major benefits are swelling reabsorption, reduced water retention, boosts weight loss, increased immune system, improved skin texture by reducing swelling, puffiness and blotches, speeding up healing scar tissue, reduced cellulite.

Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy is a holistic therapy that treats the mind, body, and spirit. It uses essential plant and flower oils for their therapeutic properties, either in massage, inhalations, or vaporisers. The most common way to use essential oils is to inhale them, both for their amazing scent and their therapeutic properties. But they can also be used in diffusers and humidifiers, as well as diluted with a carrier oil and applied to the skin. Essential oils have a wide range of therapeutic properties which can help to promote healthy sleep, relieve headaches, boost immunity, reduce stress, support digestion, promote healthy skin and alleviate pain. At Body Balance Holistic Therapies, Aromatherapy can be incorporated into the full body massage and a home treatment of blended essential oils based on the client’s individual needs can be provided.

Ace Cupping Therapy

ACE Cupping therapy is a modern form of vacuum therapy by which suction and vacuum pressure is created, which softens tight muscles and tone attachments, loosen adhesions, and lift connective tissue, bring hydration and blood flow to body tissues, move deep inflammation to the skin surface for release, and drain excess fluids and toxins by opening lymphatic pathways. This therapy is incredibly versatile, and the basic movements can easily be customized to accomplish a wide variety of techniques, from lymphatic drainage to deep tissue release.

Ear Candling

Ear Candling involves placing specialised candle in the ear canal and lightening the opposite end. This is thought to create a low-level vacuum that draws out ear wax and impurities, reliving pressure in the head and sinuses. Some of the other proposed benefits of ear candling are relieving sore throats, treating colds and flus, relieving headaches and migraines, and reducing tension and stress.
